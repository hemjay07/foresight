# In-Game Influencer Tier Audit

**Date**: December 21, 2025
**Current Count**: 61 influencers
**Purpose**: Ensure tiers reflect FANTASY SCORING POTENTIAL, not just follower count

---

## The Problem with Current Tiers

Current logic: **More followers = Higher tier = Higher cost**

But for fantasy scoring (tweets, engagement, follower growth), what matters is:
1. **Tweet frequency** - More tweets = more scoring opportunities
2. **Engagement rate** - Likes/RTs per tweet
3. **Consistency** - Daily activity vs sporadic
4. **Crypto focus** - CT native vs general account

**Example of the problem:**
- @elonmusk (229M followers, S-tier, 28 pts) - Tweets randomly, often not about crypto
- @CryptoHayes (380K followers, B-tier, 18 pts) - Arthur Hayes tweets 10x/day about crypto

Arthur Hayes will likely OUTSCORE Elon despite being 3 tiers lower. This is broken.

---

## Proper Tier Criteria

### S-Tier (28 points) - "Main Characters"
- CT natives - crypto is their PRIMARY topic
- Tweet 10+ times/day consistently
- High engagement rate (3%+ interactions)
- Their takes move markets/sentiment
- Everyone in CT follows them
- **Expected Score Range**: 80-120+ points/week

### A-Tier (22 points) - "Star Players"
- Active CT presence
- Tweet 5-10 times/day
- Good engagement, established following
- Known for quality takes
- **Expected Score Range**: 60-90 points/week

### B-Tier (18 points) - "Solid Contributors"
- Regular posters (2-5 tweets/day)
- Moderate engagement
- Good value picks
- **Expected Score Range**: 40-70 points/week

### C-Tier (12 points) - "Lottery Tickets"
- Unpredictable posting patterns
- Might go viral OR go silent
- Founders who rarely tweet
- High risk, high reward
- **Expected Score Range**: 20-60 points/week (high variance)
