# Grok Master Prompt for Influencer Research

Copy this entire prompt into Grok first to give it context, then ask follow-up questions.

---

## Initial Context Prompt (Send This First)

```
I'm building a product called Foresight - a fantasy league for Crypto Twitter.

HOW IT WORKS:
- Users draft 5 CT influencers (like Cobie, Arthur Hayes, Hsaka) within a 100-point budget
- Each influencer has a price based on their tier (S-tier costs more, C-tier is cheaper)
- Teams score based on REAL Twitter performance: follower growth, engagement, tweet activity
- Weekly contests with ETH prizes (built on Base L2)
- Free leagues now, paid tournaments coming soon (10% platform rake, 90% to winners)

THINK OF IT AS:
- Fantasy Premier League, but for Crypto Twitter personalities
- DraftKings, but for CT influencers instead of athletes
- Skill-based competition, not gambling

WE'RE LOOKING FOR INFLUENCER PARTNERS WHO:
- Have 30K-150K followers (accessible but influential)
- Are active in CT/crypto space
- Would find this concept interesting or fun
- Have engaged audiences (not bot followers)
- Might want to be featured IN the game as a draftable influencer
- Could promote to their audience for revenue share (10% of fees from referrals forever)

WE'RE OFFERING PARTNERS:
- Tier 1: 10% rev share on anyone they refer (passive income)
- Tier 2: Be featured as a draftable influencer + custom league they can monetize
- Tier 3: Advisory role with future token allocation

THE IDEAL PARTNER:
- Already talks about fantasy sports, gaming, or competitions
- Has promoted crypto projects before (but not scam promoters)
- Is a "builder" or understands products
- Would genuinely find this fun to play
- Has an audience that would enjoy competing

NOW, with this context, help me find potential partners.
```

---

## Follow-Up Prompts (After Context)

### Find Fantasy/Gaming Influencers
```
Based on the Foresight context I gave you, find CT influencers (30K-150K followers) who have tweeted about:
- Fantasy sports (FPL, DraftKings, fantasy football)
- Crypto gaming or prediction markets
- Competitions or leaderboards

For each, give me:
- Handle and follower count
- Why they'd be a good fit for Foresight
- A recent relevant tweet I can reference in my DM
- Engagement quality (do real people reply?)
```

### Find Base Ecosystem Influencers
```
Find influencers active in the Base L2 ecosystem who might like Foresight:
- Have tweeted about Base, Coinbase, or Base-native projects
- Are builders or early adopters
- 30K-150K follower range
- Would appreciate a fantasy game built on Base

Give me handles with why they'd be interested.
```

### Find "Would Actually Play" Influencers
```
Who on CT (30K-150K followers) seems like they would ACTUALLY PLAY a fantasy game about influencers? Look for people who:
- Are competitive and like games
- Talk about other CT personalities
- Engage in CT drama or discussions
- Have mentioned fantasy sports or gaming before
- Seem fun and engaged, not just broadcasting

These are people who would play Foresight for fun, not just promote it.
```

### Find Influencers Who Promoted Similar Projects
```
Who promoted or talked about Fantasy Top (the CT fantasy game) or similar crypto gaming projects?

Look for:
- People who tried it and liked the concept
- People who complained about it (potential switchers to Foresight)
- People interested in CT-based games

These are pre-qualified leads who already understand what we're building.
```

### Analyze Specific Influencer
```
Analyze @[HANDLE] as a potential Foresight partner:

1. Would they find a CT fantasy league interesting?
2. What's their engagement like? (real replies vs bots)
3. Have they promoted crypto projects before? Any red flags?
4. What's a personalization hook I could use in a DM?
5. What tier partnership would suit them? (Referral / Featured / Advisor)
6. Overall rating 1-10 as a partner prospect
```

### Find Warm Intro Opportunities
```
I want to reach out to CT influencers about Foresight. Who are "connector" accounts that:
- Know lots of other influencers
- Are approachable and reply to DMs
- Might introduce me to bigger accounts
- Are generally helpful to builders

These could be my entry point into the CT influencer network.
```

### Batch Research (Weekly)
```
Give me 10 NEW CT accounts (30K-150K followers) I should reach out to this week for Foresight partnerships.

For each:
- Handle and follower count
- Why they're relevant to a CT fantasy league
- Personalization hook for my DM
- Best tier to offer them (Referral/Featured/Advisor)

Prioritize people who seem accessible and would actually respond.
```

---

## How to Use This

1. **Start every Grok session** with the Initial Context Prompt
2. **Then ask follow-up prompts** based on what you need
3. **For each influencer found**, add to your Notion Influencer Pipeline:
   - Handle
   - Followers
   - Score (your assessment 0-100)
   - Tier (A/B/C)
   - Personalization hook
   - Notes

4. **Verify on Twitter** - Grok can hallucinate, so spot-check the accounts exist
