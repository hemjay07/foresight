# Influencer Partnership Pipeline

## Complete System for CT Influencer Outreach

**Goal**: Partner with 2-3 mid-tier CT influencers before public launch
**Timeline**: 2 weeks from start to partnerships secured
**Expected Results**: 30 outreach → 5-10 responses → 2-3 partners

---

## Phase 1: Research (Using Grok)

### Why Grok?
Grok has real-time Twitter/X access. It can:
- Find influencers by topic/engagement
- Analyze recent activity patterns
- Identify who's promoting what
- See engagement quality (real vs bots)
- Find connections between influencers

### Grok Prompts to Use

**Prompt 1: Find Fantasy/Gaming CT Influencers**
```
Find Crypto Twitter influencers (30K-150K followers) who have
tweeted about fantasy sports, DraftKings, FPL, or crypto gaming
in the past 3 months. Focus on accounts that:
- Have high engagement rates (not bot followers)
- Actually reply to their audience
- Are active in the CT community
- Have promoted crypto projects before (but not scams)

List their handles, follower count, and what makes them relevant.
```

**Prompt 2: Find Base Ecosystem Influencers**
```
Find influencers (30K-150K followers) who are active in the
Base L2 ecosystem. Look for people who:
- Have tweeted about Base, Coinbase, or Base-native projects
- Are builders or early adopters
- Have an engaged following
- Aren't just farming airdrops

List their handles with recent relevant tweets.
```

**Prompt 3: Find "Builder" Influencers**
```
Find Crypto Twitter accounts (30K-150K followers) run by people
who are also builders/founders. These are influencers who:
- Run their own projects or have in the past
- Understand product development
- Aren't just content creators
- Have credibility beyond follower count

These would be ideal advisors for a crypto fantasy gaming product.
```

**Prompt 4: Analyze Specific Influencer**
```
Analyze @[handle] for partnership potential:
- What's their engagement rate? (likes+replies / followers)
- Do they reply to their audience?
- What have they promoted in the past 6 months?
- Any red flags (scam promotions, controversy)?
- What topics do they care most about?
- Who else do they interact with frequently?
```

**Prompt 5: Find Fantasy Top Connections**
```
Who promoted or talked about Fantasy Top (the fantasy crypto
influencer game)? Find accounts that:
- Tried or promoted Fantasy Top
- Complained about Fantasy Top (potential switchers)
- Talked about fantasy gaming in crypto context

These are pre-qualified leads who already understand the concept.
```

**Prompt 6: Engagement Quality Check**
```
For @[handle], analyze their last 20 tweets:
- What's the average like/RT ratio?
- Do real accounts reply, or just bots?
- What time do they usually post?
- What content performs best for them?
- Are their followers real or purchased?
```

## Phase 2: Qualification Scorecard

### Score Each Influencer (0-100 points)

| Criteria | Points | How to Evaluate |
|----------|--------|-----------------|
| **Follower Count** | 0-15 | 30-50K=5, 50-100K=10, 100-150K=15 |
| **Engagement Rate** | 0-20 | <1%=5, 1-3%=10, 3-5%=15, >5%=20 |
| **Relevance** | 0-20 | Fantasy/gaming tweets? Base ecosystem? |
| **Accessibility** | 0-15 | DMs open? Replies to people? Active? |
| **Reputation** | 0-15 | No scam promos? Respected? Builder? |
| **Audience Fit** | 0-15 | Would their followers play Foresight? |

### Tier Classification

| Score | Tier | Approach | Offer |
|-------|------|----------|-------|
| 80-100 | A-Tier | Personal, advisor pitch | Tier 3 (Advisor) |
| 60-79 | B-Tier | Warm, partnership focus | Tier 2 (Featured) |
| 40-59 | C-Tier | Standard outreach | Tier 1 (Referral) |
| <40 | Skip | Don't waste time | - |

```
the user base. Token comes after we've proven the model works.

If advisor role interests you, happy to share more details on

```

```
  - They set entry fee

- Referral benefits (10% of referred users)

Requirements:
- Minimum 1 tweet/week about Foresight
- Stay active in their league
- 3-month initial commitment

Setup:
- Add them to influencers database
- Create their private league
- Generate referral link

```

**Tier 3: Advisor**
```
Terms:
- 1-2% token allocation (vested 12 months post-launch)

- Intro to 2-3 potential partners/users
```

## Common Objections + Responses

**"I don't promote projects"**
→ "Totally understand. If you just want to try it for yourself,
no promo needed. Here's the link - might find it fun either way."

**"What's different from Fantasy Top?"**
→ "Similar concept, different execution. We're on Base (lower
fees), scoring is fully automated from real Twitter data, and
the prize structure is simpler. Worth comparing if you tried FT."

**"I need to see traction first"**
→ "Fair. We're in early access right now, launching publicly
in [X weeks]. Happy to circle back then, or you could be one
of the early voices if you want first-mover advantage."

**"I only do paid promos"**
→ "Respect that. This isn't a paid promo ask - it's a rev share
partnership. You earn ongoing from users you bring. Different
model, potentially more lucrative long-term."

**"Sounds like gambling"**
→ "It's skill-based gaming, like DraftKings or fantasy football.
You're predicting which influencers will perform best based on
your analysis. The better your research, the better your results."

**"I don't trust crypto games"**
→ "Smart to be cautious. The contracts are on Base, you can
verify everything on Basescan. Funds go direct to winners'
wallets, not through us. Happy to walk through the contracts
if that helps."
