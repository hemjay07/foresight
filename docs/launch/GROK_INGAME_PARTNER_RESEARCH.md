## Context for Grok (Copy First)

```
I'm building Foresight - a fantasy league for Crypto Twitter.

HOW IT WORKS:
- Users draft 5 CT influencers within a 100-point budget
- Each influencer has a price based on their tier (S=28, A=22, B=18, C=12 points)
- Teams score based on REAL Twitter performance: follower growth, engagement, tweet activity
- Weekly contests with ETH prizes on Base L2

THE ANGLE: These influencers are ALREADY in our game. They're draftable players. We want to reach out and say "hey, you're a playable character in our CT fantasy league - want to promote it?"

This is WARM outreach because:
1. We've already featured them (flattering)
2. Their fans are drafting them (engaged audience)
3. They could compete with their own followers (meta)
```
